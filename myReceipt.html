Mapo tofu

Mapo tofu is a popular Chinese dish from Sichuan province. It consists of tofu set in a spicy sauce, typically a thin, oily, and bright red suspension, based on douban and douchi, along with minced meat, traditionally beef. For more information in a new window.

Ingredents
ground beef 120g
tofu one
green onions and fresh ginger one
sichuan pepper and red chilies some
vegetable oil, cooking wine, soy sacue some
salt little
Steps
  1. Combine ground beef, cooking wine, soy sauce, and ginger in a bowl. Mix well.
  2. Heat vegetable oil and sichuan pepper in a large nonstick skillet over medium high heat.
  3. When oil is hot, add ground beef for two minute, then add green onion and stir fry for another minute.
  4. Spread tofu evenly on top of ground beef.Gently mix well with dried red chilies.
  5. Gently stir a few times with a cornstarch water, until sauce thickens. Turn off heat and transfer everything to a bowl. Cover with green onion leaves.
  6. Serve warm over steamed rice or by itself as main.

Stir-fried tomato and scrambled eggs

Stir-fried tomato and scrambled eggs is a common dish in China. It is served as a main course. Because of its simplicity to prepare, it is popular to be served in student canteens as well. For more information in a new window.

Ingredents
eggs 2
tomatoes 4
green onion one
vegetable oil some
salt little
Steps
  1. Scramble eggs in a bowl with green onion, and slice tomatoes into wedges.
  2. Heat vegetable oil and fries the eggs in medium heat for 2 minutes.
  3. Add sliced tomatoes to the eggs for 5 minute.
  4. Gently mix well with salt for a minutes.
  5. Turn off heat and transfer everything to a bowl. Cover with green onion leaves.
  6. Serve warm over steamed rice or by itself as main.

Winter melon soup

Winter melon also known as Ash Gourd is quite popular in China. For more information in a new window.

Ingredents
winter melon 1 pound
pork rib 1 pound
green onion one
vegetable oil some
salt little
Steps
  1. Boil water with pork rib for 1 minutes, cool down pork rib with cold water afterwards.
  2. Boil water with winter melon.
  3. Add salt.
  4. Add pork rib for 1 hour.
  5. Turn off heat and transfer everything to a bowl. Cover with green onion leaves.
  6. Serve warm over steamed rice or by itself as main.
